About us



Opened 15 years ago, the Style Nanda brand is known for its fashion and beauty products. The Korean fashion brand features contemporary women's clothing with new arrivals every day. Style for young girls who like fashion and trendy clothes. You can easily find clothes for around $20 and some specials for more than $100. Style Nanda has many stores in Korea but the most famous is the “pink hotel” located in Myeongdong. Furthermore, our makeup brand, 3CE is one of the most popular cosmetic products for lipstick and eye makeup.





Among the product lines, 3CE is a huge success, becoming a very popular brand and considered one of the top beauty choices for women. Not only stopping at the domestic market, Stylenanda also attacks foreign markets such as: Thailand, China, Malyasia.... Especially, becoming a sub-brand of L'Oreal will help Stylenanda grow more and more. more popular all over Asia.
